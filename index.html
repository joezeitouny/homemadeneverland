Cruising through coastal Europe, one city at a time

We’re Joe & Tamara. A young couple with big ambitions. Recently we set ourselves on our biggest adventure yet. Get a sailboat and sail it all the way from the Netherlands down to the Mediterranean Sea. On this website we’ll be posting content of our journey along the way.

It may sound impossible and a lot would consider it crazy, but in 2020 we’ve set our minds on sailing from the Netherlands to the Mediterranean sea while stopping at the coastal cities & towns along the way.
